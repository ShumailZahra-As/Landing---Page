The Morning Dew

In the morning light so fresh and new,
The world awakens with the dew.
Each droplet tells a tale untold,
Of nature's beauty, pure and bold.

The sun peeks over the horizon's line,
Casting rays that warmly shine.
Birds begin their morning song,
A melody that carries on.

The flowers open, petals wide,
Welcoming the day with pride.
The morning breeze gently blows,
Whispering secrets the earth knows.

As the world stirs from night's embrace,
A smile spreads across its face.
For in the morning's gentle hue,
There's a promise of life anew.

With every dawn, a fresh start,
A chance to heal, a mending heart.
The morning dew, so pure, so clear,
Brings hope and joy with each new year.
