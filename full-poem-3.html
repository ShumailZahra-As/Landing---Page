Echoes of the Night

In the stillness of the night,
Echoes whisper, soft and light.
Shadows dance beneath the moon,
A silent symphony, a timeless tune.

Stars adorn the velvet sky,
Glimmering like a firefly.
The world in slumber, calm and deep,
Secrets in the darkness keep.

Owls hoot in distant trees,
Carried by the gentle breeze.
Winds murmur through the leaves,
Tales of yore, the mind believes.

The night unfolds its quiet grace,
A serene and sacred space.
In dreams we wander, free and far,
Guided by a shooting star.

As dawn approaches, soft and slow,
The night prepares to let go.
Echoes fade with morning's light,
But memories linger, through day and night.
