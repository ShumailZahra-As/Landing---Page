Whispers of the Wind

The wind whispers secrets, soft and low,
Through valleys deep, where flowers grow.
It carries tales from far and wide,
On gentle breezes, it will glide.

It stirs the leaves in a rhythmic dance,
A symphony of nature, a fleeting trance.
The whispers tell of distant lands,
Of golden shores and desert sands.

It moves through forests, wild and free,
Rustling the branches of ancient trees.
With every gust, a story unfolds,
Of whispered dreams and truths untold.

The wind whispers secrets, old and new,
A timeless song, forever true.
Listen closely, and you may hear,
The wind's soft voice, so pure and clear.
